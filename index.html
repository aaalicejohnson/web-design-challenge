City Latitude and Weather

This project analyzed how weather changes as you get closer to the equator. We accomplished this by pulling over 500 cites at random from OpenWeatherMap API.


Once we had our dataset, we used Matplotlib to plot various aspects of weather vs. city latitude. The weather factors analyzed include max temperature, humidity, cloudiness, and wind speed. This website provides source data as well as plots with descriptions of trends and correaltions that were found in the analysis.
